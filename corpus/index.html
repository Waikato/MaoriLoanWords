NZT Corpus


The New Zealand Twitter Corpus (NZT Corpus) is a diachronic corpus consisting of two components:

  1. Labelled Corpus: 3,685 manually-coded Tweets which include at least one MƒÅori word of interest.
  2. Processed Corpus: Just under 1.2 million Tweets that were classified as relevant, using the Labelled Corpus as training data.
